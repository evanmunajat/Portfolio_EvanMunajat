Clothing Brand Case Study

Photoshop | Iluslator | Notion

Overview

GENZIQUE is a streetwear brand born from the anxiety of monotonous mainstream fashion. The goal is to create fashion products that are bold, limited, and relevant to today's Gen Z lifestyle.

Problem Statement

Many young people find it difficult to find fashion that authentically reflects their identity. On the other hand, big brands focus too much on global trends and ignore local roots and expressions.

Solution

Limited drop per collection, Bold designs that reflect youth subculture, Collaboration with local artists and communities, Visual campaigns that are consistent and relate to the target audience.
